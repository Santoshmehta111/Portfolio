Bill-Splitter


The Bill-Splitter is a Web Application software designed to streamline bill-related processes with friends.
addresses the challenges posed by manual bill splitting and aims to enhance efficiency and accuracy.
The Bill-Splitter project encompasses the following key features:
⦁

Expense Management Module:
⦁ Allow users to create and categorize expenses, including the amount and date.
⦁ Enable users to edit or delete expenses as needed.
⦁
Expense Splitting Module:
⦁ Offer the option to split expenses equally among participants.
⦁ Automatically calculate the split amounts based on the chosen method.
⦁
Settlement Module:
⦁ Track balances owed between participants based on the split expenses.
⦁ Provide options for settling balances, such as balance settled.
